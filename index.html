Privacy Policy for Cosmic Companions

Thank you for using Cosmic Companions. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection and Use

Cosmic Companions is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data with any third parties beyond what is necessary for the core functionality of the app.

All user data is saved locally on your device. No data is uploaded, transmitted, or exposed externally except in the limited case described below.

2. Use of OpenAI API

To provide AI-powered responses, Cosmic Companions sends data to OpenAI's API. This includes:

  • The messages you write to communicate with the AI.
  • The name or nickname you choose to be called.
  • Any optional information you provide to personalize the chatbot experience, such as your interests, job, and age (if you choose to fill out those fields).

This information is only used to help the chatbot provide more engaging and meaningful conversations. It is not stored by us or used for any purpose beyond enhancing your experience within the app.

Please review OpenAI’s Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle data.

3. Data Storage

All app-related data (such as conversation history and user preferences) is stored only on your device. We do not have access to it, nor is it uploaded to any external servers.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties. The only third-party service used is OpenAI, as described above, and only for the purpose of generating responses to your inputs.

5. User Control

Since all data is stored locally:

  • You can delete the app to remove all stored information.
  • There are no user accounts, tracking tools, or hidden data collection mechanisms in this app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted within the app or on the app store listing. Continued use of the app after changes implies acceptance of the new terms.
